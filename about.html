Who am I?

Hey! I am a tattoo artist based in Brno who focuses on dotwork.

I tattoo in my own personal style that consists of minimalistic, dotwork and anaglyph tattoos.

All my tattoos are done via handpoke technique, which means I do not use any tattoo machines. This technique allows me to create unique and detailed designs while ensuring a more personal and intimate experience for my clients.

If you are interested in getting a tattoo or have any questions, feel free to reach out to me through the contact page or book a session directly.
